The Everyday Rider

Whether commuting to work or riding to college, small mistakes happen every day. A forgotten strap, a helmet worn loose, a motorcycle started before safety is confirmed. PalaVita detects unsecured chin straps, activates audible alerts before the journey begins, and continues alerting until the issue is resolved — no override, no snooze. Because every ride, however routine, is only as safe as the rider who starts it.

Delivery riders operate under constant time pressure — making them the most likely to skip safety steps. PalaVita detects motorcycle movement and absent helmet usage simultaneously, activating immediate warnings before the journey continues.
